How it works

Upload, choose a role, then decide your next move.

FixSend asks for a PDF, DOCX, or TXT CV and a target role. It checks the CV against role expectations, gives a readiness score and a clear decision, then offers a deeper Fix Pack dashboard for applicants who want more structured rewrite guidance before applying.
